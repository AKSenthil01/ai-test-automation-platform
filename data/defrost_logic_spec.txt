Defrost Control Logic Specification

The defrost system removes ice buildup from the evaporator coil.

Defrost Trigger Conditions

Defrost cycle can start when:

1. Compressor runtime exceeds configured threshold
2. Evaporator temperature remains below freezing for long duration
3. Scheduled defrost timer expires

---

Defrost Sequence

Step 1
Controller stops compressor.

Step 2
Evaporator fan stops.

Step 3
Defrost heater activates.

Step 4
Ice melts from evaporator coil.

---

Defrost Termination Conditions

Defrost cycle stops when:

1. Evaporator temperature exceeds termination threshold
2. Maximum defrost time is reached

---

Post Defrost Recovery

After defrost completion:

1. Heater turns off
2. System waits for drip delay
3. Compressor restarts
4. Cooling resumes

---

Defrost Failure Conditions

Possible causes of defrost failure:

* Heater malfunction
* Temperature sensor failure
* Incorrect defrost timing configuration

Controller generates alarm if defrost does not terminate properly.
