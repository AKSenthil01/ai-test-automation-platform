A2L Refrigerant Leak Detection Specification

A2L refrigerants are mildly flammable and require leak detection
for safety compliance.

Leak Detection System

The controller continuously monitors signals from gas sensors.

Sensors detect refrigerant concentration in the environment.

---

Normal Operation

Sensor readings remain below safety threshold.

Controller continues normal operation.

---

Leak Detection Condition

Leak alarm triggers when refrigerant concentration
exceeds configured safety limit.

Typical threshold:
25% of Lower Flammability Limit (LFL)

---

Controller Response To Leak

When leak detected:

1. Compressor shuts down immediately
2. Fans activate to ventilate area
3. Alarm status activated
4. Event logged in controller memory

---

Reset Condition

System can return to normal operation when:

1. Gas concentration drops below safe limit
2. Manual reset performed by technician

---

Possible Fault Conditions

False leak detection may occur due to:

* Sensor drift
* Electrical noise
* Sensor calibration error

Regular sensor calibration recommended.
