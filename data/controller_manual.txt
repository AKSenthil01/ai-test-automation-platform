Refrigeration Controller Overview

The refrigeration controller manages compressor operation,
fan control, defrost cycles, and safety protection mechanisms.

Main Components Controlled

1. Compressor
   Controls refrigeration cycle by compressing refrigerant gas.

2. Evaporator Fan
   Maintains airflow across evaporator coil.

3. Condenser Fan
   Dissipates heat from condenser.

4. Temperature Sensors
   Monitor evaporator temperature and ambient temperature.

---

Controller Operating Modes

Cooling Mode
Compressor runs to maintain target temperature.

Defrost Mode
Controller temporarily stops cooling to remove frost
from evaporator coil.

Alarm Mode
Activated when abnormal conditions occur.

---

Safety Protections

High Discharge Temperature Protection
Compressor shuts down if discharge temperature exceeds limit.

Low Suction Pressure Protection
Prevents compressor damage due to refrigerant loss.

Sensor Failure Protection
Controller enters safe mode if sensor input is invalid.

---

Communication Interfaces

BACnet MS/TP
Used for building automation system integration.

Modbus RTU
Used for monitoring and configuration.

Controller logs all major events and faults for diagnostics.
